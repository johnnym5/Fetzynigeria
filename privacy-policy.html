Privacy Policy

This page is the dedicated destination for privacy and personal data handling information across the site. It complements legal.html, which remains the overview page for company registration and compliance matters.

Data handling overview

FETZY NIGERIA LIMITED limits the collection of personal data to information required to respond to enquiries, manage project relationships, recruit talent, and comply with applicable legal obligations. Information submitted through contact or hiring channels is used only for legitimate business purposes and retained for no longer than necessary.

Where required, the company aligns its internal practices with applicable Nigerian privacy and data protection requirements, uses reasonable organisational safeguards, and restricts access to authorised personnel and service providers acting on its behalf.

Contact for privacy matters

Questions about privacy, data requests, or corrections may be directed to the company through its published contact channels until a dedicated data protection contact is formally published.
