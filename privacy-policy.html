Data Protection

Privacy Policy.

How Fetzy Nigeria Limited collects, secures, and manages your personal information.

1. Information Collection

We collect information that you voluntarily provide when engaging with our services, including:

  • Identificaton Data: Name, job title, and company name.
  • Contact Channels: Email addresses, phone numbers, and physical office locations.
  • Project Details: Architectural requirements, budget estimates, and site locations.
  • Usage Data: Technical details regarding your interaction with our digital platforms.

2. Use of Information

Your data is processed exclusively for professional purposes, including:

  • Providing requested project consultations and technical evaluations.
  • Managing procurement and supply chain communications.
  • Fulfilling legal and regulatory reporting requirements.
  • Sending project updates and corporate announcements (with your consent).

3. Data Security Measures

Fetzy Nigeria Limited implements industry-leading security protocols to prevent unauthorized access or disclosure of your data. This includes end-to-end encryption for digital transmissions and restricted physical access to our servers. We maintain strict NDPR (Nigeria Data Protection Regulation) compliance across all our operational nodes.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others. We may share generic aggregated demographic information not linked to any personal identification information regarding visitors and users with our business partners, trusted affiliates, and advertisers for the purposes outlined above.

5. Your Digital Rights

Under Nigerian law, you have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request corrections to any inaccurate information.
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time.
  • Request the deletion of your data from our systems (subject to legal retention requirements).
